Our Story

Aquanics was born from a simple frustration — finding quality aquarium fish and pet supplies in Bhubaneswar was hard. We set out to change that by building a platform that brings ethically sourced, healthy live fish and premium pet products directly to your doorstep.

From colorful Bettas and graceful Goldfish to parrots, dogs, and everything they need — we're your one-stop pet destination in Odisha.

We deliver across Bhubaneswar, Puri, Cuttack & Khordha with same-day processing and careful live animal packaging.
