🐠 Dead on Arrival (DOA) Policy

We guarantee the safe delivery of all live fish and animals. If your order arrives with dead livestock, we will replace it — no questions asked.

What qualifies as DOA?

  • Fish/animals that are dead upon opening the package
  • Fish that die within 24 hours of delivery due to transit stress
  • Animals that arrive visibly sick or injured from shipping

How to claim DOA

  • Take a clear photo/video of the dead fish in the unopened bag within 2 hours of delivery
  • Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 24 hours of delivery
  • Share your Order ID, photo, and a brief description
  • We will ship a replacement at no additional cost within 3–5 business days

What is NOT covered

  • Death due to improper tank conditions (wrong pH, temperature, ammonia spikes)
  • Death after 24 hours of delivery
  • Damage caused by improper acclimatisation

↩️ Returns & Refunds

We accept returns on non-living products within 7 days of delivery if they are unused and in original packaging.

Eligible for return

  • Aquarium equipment (filters, pumps, lights) — unused and in original packaging
  • Pet food — unopened, undamaged packaging
  • Accessories and toys — unused, with tags

Not eligible for return

  • Live fish, birds, and animals (see DOA policy for livestock)
  • Opened food or medicine
  • Custom or personalised items
  • Items damaged by the customer

Refund timeline

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

🚚 Shipping Policy

Free delivery on orders above ₹599. Standard delivery fee of ₹49 for orders below ₹599.

Delivery timelines

  • Regular products: 3–7 business days
  • Live fish & animals: 1–3 business days (we use specialised live fish transport)
  • Metro cities: usually 1–2 days faster

Live fish shipping

  • Fish are packed in oxygen-filled bags with thermal insulation
  • We ship live fish Monday–Friday to avoid weekend delays at courier hubs
  • Tracking details will be shared via WhatsApp/email once dispatched

Areas we ship to

We currently ship to all major cities and towns across India. Some remote PIN codes may have extended delivery times.
